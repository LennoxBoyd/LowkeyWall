Privacy Policy

At LowkeyWall, your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains what minimal data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-submitted confessions and topics
  • Optional flags to request an AI-generated response
  • Upvote interactions (stored anonymously)
  • Basic non-identifiable analytics (e.g. page views, device type)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to run the platform — publish confessions, show trending topics, allow upvotes, and optionally provide AI-generated replies. We never sell your data or use it for advertising outside LowkeyWall.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to keep the limited data secure. However, please note that no online service can guarantee 100% security.

4. Your Rights

Confessions are anonymous by design — we do not collect personal accounts. If you wish to remove content you posted, please contact us with details of the post.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be posted on this page.

Questions? Please reach out via our Contact Page.
